Marina Barrage

Marina Barrage is an iconic landmark that we all already know. Built in 2008, this dam has become one of our favorite spots for picnic and cute outdoor dates. If you haven't been there, visited this sloping green roof, what are you waiting for!

Visit the Sustainable Singapore Gallery


ArtsScience Museum may be our go-to spot for artsy exhibits, the Sustainable Singapore Gallery is also worthy of a visit, especially since you can access it for free. If you have green-fingers (or you simply want an air-conditioned area to escape the heat), give this gallery a try!

Use it as a pitstop while cycling at the bay area


Aside from offering a myriad of recreation activities and facilities, Marina Barrage is also closely connected to PCNs and other trails, making it bike-friendly! There are also bike rental shops nearby if you do not have your own bicycle. If you and your loved ones fancy a fitspo outing, cycling at the bay area is definitely what you should do.You can always spend your time at Marina Barrage flying some kites too! You can roam and run around the green slope as much as you want!
